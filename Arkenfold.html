THE CONSTITUTION

THE CONSTITUTION OF THE DOMINION OF ARKENFOLD

Ratified to Establish Absolute Order, Enduring Strength, and Unchallenged Authority.

PREAMBLE

We, the People of Arkenfold, united by will and bound by purpose, establish this Dominion to secure order, strength, and survival in a world of conflict and chaos.
Through unwavering authority, disciplined unity, and resolute command, we shall endure, expand, and prevail.

ARTICLE I – THE DOMINION

The Dominion of Arkenfold is a sovereign state founded upon absolute authority, unity of command, and total national strength.

All institutions, offices, and forces serve the Dominion and its Supreme Archon.

The Dominion exists to ensure survival, dominance, and the perpetual stability of its people.

ARTICLE II – THE SUPREME ARCHON

The Supreme Archon is the highest authority of the Dominion.

The Supreme Archon holds ultimate command over law, military, governance, and national policy.

The word of the Supreme Archon carries the force of law and requires no approval or ratification.

ARTICLE III – THE HIGH COMMAND COUNCIL

The Supreme Archon shall be advised by the High Command Council.

The Council shall consist of:

  • The Grand Marshal
  • The High Chancellor
  • The Minister of Dominion
  • The Overseer of Unity

The Council advises but does not limit the authority of the Supreme Archon.

ARTICLE IV – LAW AND ORDER

Order is the highest principle of the Dominion.

All citizens are bound to obey the laws of the Dominion and the commands of its authorities.

Acts of treason, rebellion, sabotage, or defiance shall be met with decisive punishment.

ARTICLE V – THE MILITARY

The military of the Dominion shall be known as the Arkenfold Iron Legion.

The Iron Legion exists to defend the Dominion, enforce its will, and expand its influence.

The Grand Marshal commands all military forces under the authority of the Supreme Archon.

ARTICLE VI – UNITY AND LOYALTY

Loyalty to the Dominion and the Supreme Archon is the highest civic duty.

All citizens shall contribute to the strength and stability of the state through labor, service, or obedience.

Dissent that threatens unity shall be suppressed in defense of national survival.

ARTICLE VII – EXPANSION AND SECURITY

The Dominion reserves the right to expand its influence, territory, and control in the interest of stability and security.

Foreign entities that threaten Arkenfold may be neutralized through political, economic, or military means.

ARTICLE VIII – AMENDMENTS

This Constitution may be amended only by the will of the Supreme Archon.

No amendment may weaken the authority of the Dominion or its Supreme Archon.

ARTICLE IX – FOUNDING SEAL

Let this Constitution stand as the unbreakable foundation of the Dominion —
A testament to strength,
A fortress of order,
And the eternal command of Arkenfold.

SIGNED AND ENACTED BY: Supreme Archon Imperius Ruler of the Dominion of Arkenfold
